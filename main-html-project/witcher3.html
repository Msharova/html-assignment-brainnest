About The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt

The Witcher Trilogy

The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt is a 2015 action role-playing game developed and published by CD Projekt Red. It is the sequel to the 2011 game The Witcher 2: Assassins of Kings and the third game in The Witcher video game series, played in an open world with a third-person perspective. The games follow The Witcher series of fantasy novels written by Andrzej Sapkowski.
